What is StartupFuel?

StartupFuel is a fundraising platform for Canadian entrepreneurs. We help startups raise money for their ideas and promote their brands.

How Does it Work?

We curate our list of startups, working with them to come up with a cool list of rewards for donating. We then manage those donations, passing them on directy to the startup (less the ~2-3% Paypal cut).

StartupFuel was developed entirely from concept to working product at Startup Weekend 2011 by 6 dudes:

@mattskilly @defrex @trevguy
@maxmeilleur @jwpeddle @lylepstein

Hosting and related services are provided by Hipsell free of charge as a service to the Canadian startup community.
