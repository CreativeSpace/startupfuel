Pitch Us Your Startup!

StartupFuel was built and launched in 54 hours at Startup Weekend Toronto in 2011. We're still figuring things out, but if you'd like to promote your startup on StartupFuel, please send us your pitch information. We promote a maximum of 4 startups at any given time, so if all the slots are taken you'll be added to our queue.

Please be patient with us as this is not our full time job, but rather a project we'd like to keep alive for the Canadian startup community. If you're a Django/Python developer and would like to work on this project, please message us @startupfuelca or DM us on Twitter. Thank you very much and good luck.

Pitch Details

Follow the instructions below as closely as possible; if we don't receive your pitch to spec there's a very good chance your page won't be posted.

  • 460 x 200 banner (featured banner for landing page)
  • 580 x 150 logo (max logo area for pitch page - will be on white back)
  • One liner / 30 second elevator pitch text (max 300 characters)
  • Twitter link/handle, Facebook link, and domain
  • YouTube video link (recommended) or 580 x 350 image (if no video)
  • Incentives (minimum 5 e.g., $10 - A XYZ logo tee...)
  • Goal amount (e.g., $10k)
  • PayPal info (username, email)
  • Contact name and phone number
  • Business name and address

Note: All sizes are in pixels.

Email your pitch to pitch@startupfuel.ca

Be sure to follow us on Twitter @startupfuelca as we're likely to @ you when you're live.
